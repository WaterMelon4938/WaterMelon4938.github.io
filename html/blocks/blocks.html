Blocks

Dirt
Dirt is a block found abundantly in most biomes under a layer of grass blocks at the top of the Overworld.
Sand
Sand is a gravity-affected block found abundantly in deserts and beaches, and less commonly in or near surface water.
Stone
Stone is a block found underground in the Overworld or on the surface of mountains.
Grass
Grass is a non-solid plant block that has wheat seeds as a drop. Its color is biome-dependent, and matches the color of grass blocks.
Snow
Snow, known as Top Snow in Minecraft for the New Nintendo 3DS, is a ground cover block found on the surface in snowy biomes, and can be replenished during snowfall.
